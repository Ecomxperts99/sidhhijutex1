Welcome to Siddhi Jutex

Leading Manufacturer Since 1995

Siddhi Jutex Creative Enterprises specializes in manufacturing premium quality jute products for wholesale and export markets. With over 25 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility produces eco-friendly jute bags, hessian cloth, and industrial packaging solutions that meet international quality standards.

Our Vision & Mission

Building a Sustainable Future

Our vision is to be the global leader in eco-friendly jute products, promoting sustainable practices and reducing plastic usage worldwide. We believe in creating products that are not just functional but also contribute to environmental conservation.

Through continuous innovation, quality excellence, and customer-centric approach, we aim to make jute the preferred choice for packaging and textile solutions across industries.
